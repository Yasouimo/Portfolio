About RADEM

RADEM, or the Autonomous Water and Electricity Distribution Authority of Meknes (Régie Autonome de Distribution d'Eau et d'Électricité de Meknès), was a Moroccan public utility company that provided water, electricity, and wastewater services to the city of Meknes and surrounding areas. Founded in 1969, RADEM served as a crucial infrastructure provider for decades before evolving into the Société Régionale Multiservices Fès-Meknès (SRM-FM) in recent years. The company played a vital role in Morocco's utility infrastructure development and modernization efforts in the Fès-Meknès region.
